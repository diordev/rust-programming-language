# Release Channels

## Short Definition

Rust release channels — stable, beta, va nightly train modeli bo'yicha yuradigan release qatlamlari.

## Why It Matters

Bu model bir vaqtning o'zida experimentation, regression testing, va stable reliability'ni boshqaradi.

## Mental Model

Feature avval nightly'da yashaydi, keyin beta orqali sayohat qiladi, oxirida stable'ga yetadi.

## Syntax and Examples

- `nightly`
- `beta`
- `stable`

## Common Mistakes

- Beta va nightly bir xil risk profiliga ega deb o'ylash.
- Stable'ning support window'i indefinite deb o'ylash.
