# Stability Without Stagnation

## Short Definition

Rustning guiding principle'i: stable userlar upgrade'dan qo'rqmasin, lekin til va compiler ham doim rivojlanib borsin.

## Why It Matters

Bu prinsip editionlar, release channels, feature flags, va RFC process'ning orqasidagi strategik fikrni tushuntiradi.

## Mental Model

"Painless upgrades + continuous progress" — ikkisini birga saqlash.

## Syntax and Examples

Bu syntax emas, release policy va project philosophy.

## Common Mistakes

- Stability bo'lsa experimentation bo'lmaydi deb o'ylash.
- Experimental featurelar bor ekan, stable upgrade xavfli bo'ladi deb o'ylash.
